Sweet Treats is created by chocaholics for chocaholics, it was founded by Lindy Mojapelo and Mpho Khoele in 2018. They combined their diverse backgrounds to the create this heaven for people with sweet teeth. Lindy who is a pastry chef and Mpho who is a chocolatier teamed up to create amazing cakes, desserts and delicious treats.



We are based in the Mall of Africa, unit 34B.
